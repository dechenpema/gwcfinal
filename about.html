    About Us

    As New Yorkers, we recognize how fortunate we are to have amazing educational opportunities and participate in programs like Girls Who Code. However, we know that others within our city and across the country might not understand the resources available to them, while the government does not provide adequate educational support to the most disadvantaged communities. Disparities in funding, support, and therefore educational quality hinder many Americans from achieving their full potential.
